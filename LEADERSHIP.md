## 📘 CTO Operating System
My operational governance system for building and scaling technology organizations.

The CTO Operating System is **modular and maturity-aware**.  
Governance practices are activated progressively depending on the stage of the company (startup → growth → scale).

This allows early teams to remain lightweight and fast, while operational discipline naturally increases as the organization grows.

This Operating System is the implementation layer of this portfolio:  
while the Portfolio defines vision and strategy, the **Operating System** codifies day-to-day engineering, architecture, security, and reliability standards in production environments.

It includes development process, QA, security, onboarding, and incident management standards.
It provides the governance layer that allows teams to scale execution without losing speed, clarity, or control.

📁 Repository → [CTO Operating System (Operational Governance & Engineering Standards)](https://github.com/matdup/cto-operating-system)
